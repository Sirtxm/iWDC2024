3Rs

The concept and approach for the efficient use of available resources can help
reduce the amount of waste by focusing on reducing usage, reusing, and recycling waste.

Re-card

Let me explain what reuse, reduces and recycle is.
And We will explain the advantages of each.

Recycle
Recycling involves processing or improving the quality of waste or used materials to restore them to their original.
Reuse
Reusing waste, packaging, or leftover materials without undergoing processing or transformation.
Reduce
Reducing resource consumption is the first step because it is the easiest and most effective method.

DASHBOARD

Using data on plastic waste from Japan and Thailand,
we will introduce the differences in waste disposal methods between the two countries.

the data on plastic waste over the past 9 years for Japan and Thailand Japan shows a declining trend year by year, while Thailand shows an increasing and decreasing trend.
It is evident that the graphs do not overlap due to the differing population sizes of Japan and Thailand.

From the graph, it is clear that the amount of plastic waste is quite high. After a few years, the graph shows a gradual decline.
However, between 2016 and 2018, the graph remains relatively stable.
In 2019, plastic waste began to decrease gradually due to effective waste management practices.

The graph shows fluctuating trends, peaking in 2019 due to China's restrictions on waste raw material imports,
which left plastic waste without export destinations.
However, improvements in waste management since then have significantly reduced plastic waste.
